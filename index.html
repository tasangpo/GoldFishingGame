⚓ GOLD FISHING GAME ⚓

Welcome to the Gold Fishing Game! Instructions are very simple, use you "A" key to move your player left and "D" key to move your player right. When you think the time is right, enter your "space" key to drop your line to pick up the gold. Be careful though, if the shark touches your line or by some stroke of astronomical bad luck, you hit the shark on the head, it will climb up and eat you!! Good luck player, hit the start button below to play!
